# 🤖 CICR – Creative and Innovative Cell in Robotics

Welcome to the official GitHub of CICR – the Creative and Innovative Cell in Robotics at JIIT 128! 🚀  
We are a passionate, multidisciplinary innovation society committed to building next-generation robotics, AI/ML projects, hardware prototypes, and web technologies.

---

## 🌟 About Us

- Part of Jaypee Youth Club (JYC) at JIIT 128, operating also as an independent lab.
- A highly equipped lab space with cutting-edge tools, hardware, and development environments.
- Driven by a robust team:  
  Volunteers (1st Year) → Core Team (2nd Year) → Coordinators (3rd Year) → Heads & Advisors (4th Year).

We work across:  
🔹 Robotics & Embedded Systems  
🔹 AI, Machine Learning & Data Science  
🔹 Web & App Development  
🔹 Hardware Design & IoT  
🔹 Product Innovation & Prototyping  

---

## 🛠️ What We Do

- Build and maintain technical projects for competitions, research, and real-world applications.
- Host workshops, events, and hackathons to nurture technical excellence in the community.
- Represent CICR at national and international competitions.
- Collaborate across disciplines to push the boundaries of innovation.
